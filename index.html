How it works

1. Join as a WTF member

Choose a monthly or yearly membership and complete age verification where required by law.

Members must follow all local, state, and federal regulations for alcohol and firearms.

2. Find nearby partners

Use the WTF app to locate cigar lounges, whiskey bars, ranges, and certified instructors near you.

All firearm-related partners are licensed businesses — no private sellers.

3. Generate one-time vouchers

Before checkout, generate a single-use code that your vendor checks and redeems.

Each code is tracked in the system and cannot be reused or resold.

For licensed partners & legal safety

WTF is built for legitimate businesses.

Cigar lounges, whiskey venues, ranges, certified instructors, and licensed firearms retailers can promote in-person offers to verified members. All firearm-related ads must come from licensed FFLs or ranges – never from private individuals.
